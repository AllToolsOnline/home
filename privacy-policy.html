Privacy Policy for AllToolsOnline

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

Your privacy is critically important to us. At AllToolsOnline, we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don't ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don't share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We don't store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of one of our services.
  • All tools on AllToolsOnline are designed to perform their functions client-side in your browser. This means your files and data are processed directly on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. This ensures your privacy and security.

This Privacy Policy applies to the website AllToolsOnline.github.io (the "Website").

1. Information We Collect

Because AllToolsOnline operates primarily as a client-side application, we collect very limited personal information.

  • No Personal Data Uploaded: When you use tools like the QR Code Generator or Image to PDF Converter, your input data (text, images) is processed directly in your web browser. It is never sent to our servers.
  • Non-Personal Information: Like most website operators, AllToolsOnline collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Our purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how AllToolsOnline's visitors use its website.
  • Aggregated Statistics: AllToolsOnline may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its Website. For instance, AllToolsOnline may monitor the most popular tools or the number of conversions performed (without knowing what was converted). This data is aggregated and does not identify any individual user.

2. How We Use Information

  • To Operate and Maintain the Website: We use the non-personal, aggregated information to understand website usage patterns and to improve the performance and features of AllToolsOnline.
  • No Data Storage for Tools: Since your data is processed client-side, we do not store any of your input files or generated output from our tools.

3. Protection of Certain Non-Personal Information

AllToolsOnline takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and non-personally-identifying information. However, as we primarily handle non-personally identifying information, the risk is minimal.

4. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. AllToolsOnline may use cookies to help identify and track visitors, their usage of the Website, and their Website access preferences. AllToolsOnline visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using AllToolsOnline's Website, with the drawback that certain features of the Website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

5. Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, AllToolsOnline may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in AllToolsOnline's sole discretion. AllToolsOnline encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
